演示账号申请流程

“BIM演示系统”是万达集团部署的，用于展示万达筑云项目管理平台操作功能的模拟系统。主要目的用于向广大万达集团合作伙伴提供模拟操作环境，以熟悉万达集团万达筑云项目管理平台的功能及操作。

“BIM演示系统”的登录权限由万达集团统一控制，如各合作伙伴有登录演示系统熟悉操作的需要，可通过电子邮件的方式联系与贵方对接的万达集团内部业务部门进行申请，并统一开通。账号开通后15日内有效，过期后自动失效。

Account Application for Demo System

Developed by Wanda Group, BIM Demo System simulates Wanda BIM General Contracting Management Platform where main contractors split contracts and manage subcontracts. We aim at providing the virtual operating environment for our mass group of partners to get familiar with the functions and operation of Wanda BIM General Contracting Management Platform.

Wanda Group is the only manager and owner of the permissions to log in the demo system. To open the accounts for logging, please contact our related business departments which are responsible for communicating with you via email. The accounts will be valid within 15 days pending their opening and expire automatically afterwards.

You are welcome to use the system and send your constructive opinions.
